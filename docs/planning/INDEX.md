# Flip-ly Strategic Planning — The Pivot

**Created:** 2026-04-04
**Status:** In Progress
**Decision Authority:** Keith (founder)

---

## Context

Flip-ly launched as a garage sale aggregator. The core engine works — multi-source scraping, AI scoring, weekly digests, search. But the garage-sale-only positioning caps the business at a seasonal, casual-use product with low willingness to pay.

**The pivot:** Garage sales become a feature, not the product. The product is **"Find underpriced stuff near you before anyone else"** — source-agnostic local deal discovery powered by AI scoring.

This folder contains the analytical foundation for that pivot. Every decision gets documented. Every number gets verified. No vibes.

| 1 | 2026-04-04 | Pivot from garage-sale-only to local deal discovery platform | Garage sales alone = seasonal, low WTP, low ceiling. Broader positioning = year-round value, higher WTP, scalable | Stay garage-sale-only (rejected: $3-5 ceiling), build totally new product (rejected: engine already works) |
